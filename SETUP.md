# Network Troubleshooter Simulator - Gameplay Instructions

Welcome to the **Network Troubleshooter Simulator**! This guide will help you to navigate the gameplay and effectively troubleshoot network issues.

## Gameplay Overview
The goal of the game is to diagnose and resolve various network problems in a simulated environment. Players will encounter different scenarios and must apply their troubleshooting skills to resolve them.

## Getting Started

1. **Launch the Game**: Open the Network Troubleshooter Simulator application on your device.
2. **Select a Scenario**: From the main menu, choose a troubleshooting scenario to play. Scenarios vary in difficulty and complexity.

## Troubleshooting Steps

- **Identify the Problem**: Each scenario will present symptoms of a network issue. Read the clues carefully.
- **Use Tools**: Utilize the available tools in the game to diagnose the problem. Common tools include:
  - Ping
  - Traceroute
  - IP Config
- **Apply Fixes**: Based on the diagnosis, apply the necessary fixes. This may include resetting devices, changing configurations, or following troubleshooting protocols.
- **Test the Solution**: After applying fixes, always test to ensure that the network issue is resolved.

## Scoring
Players earn points based on accuracy and speed in resolving issues. Higher difficulty scenarios yield more points.

## Tips for Success
- **Take Notes**: Keep track of the solutions for recurring problems.
- **Practice Different Scenarios**: The more you play, the better you’ll understand various network issues.

## Conclusion
Good luck, and enjoy your journey to becoming a network troubleshooting expert!
